About

The Beatles is an English rock band formed in 1960, consisting of John Lennon, Paul McCartney, George Harrison and Ringo Starr. Their sound incorporated elements of classical music and traditional pop in innovative ways. They have also explored other music styles from folk & Indian music to psychedelia and hard rock.
